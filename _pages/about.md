I am a Ph.D. candidate in Marketing at the Tepper School of Business, Carnegie Mellon University. My research examines how artificial intelligence reshapes managerial decision-making, consumer search, and platform markets. I combine structural modeling, machine learning, reinforcement learning, and generative AI to study how firms, consumers, and platforms make decisions in digital environments.
